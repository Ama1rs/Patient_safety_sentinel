# Patient_safety_sentinel
A real-time public health surveillance and patient safety engine. Uses vector databases and Explainable AI (XAI) to monitor social streams for suicide signals, adverse drug events (ADRs), and emerging communal health trends.

Core Mission
To transform unstructured social data into actionable safety signals, prioritizing immediate intervention for at-risk individuals and identifying systemic risks in drug safety and communal health.

Key Features
1. Signal Detection & Critical Alerts
Suicide Prevention: Real-time semantic analysis to identify early-warning signs and high-risk distress signals.

Pharmacovigilance (ADR): Automated flagging of adverse drug reactions, treatment dissatisfaction, and potential medication misuse.

Misinformation Guard: Identification of "miracle cure" claims and fraudulent medical advice trending in social ecosystems.

2. Multi-Dimensional Monitoring
Custom Keyword Injection: Dynamic tracking for specific brand names, generics, and emerging symptoms.

Geospatial Intelligence: Location-based sorting to detect localized "communal spreads" or regional health spikes (e.g., seasonal respiratory trends).

Platform Agnostic: Integrated connectors for YouTube, X (Twitter), forums, and niche healthcare communities.

3. Investigative UI & Explainability
Thread Visualization: Node-link graphs to visualize how health rumors or safety concerns propagate through digital networks.

Explainable AI (XAI): Transparent summaries explaining why a specific signal was flagged, ensuring clinical trust.

Trend Fusion: Integration with Google Trends to contextualize internal data against global search volume.

Technical Architecture
The system follows an "Object-Oriented Intelligence" path:

Ingestion: Real-time streaming from configured social APIs.

Vectorization: Text is converted into high-dimensional embeddings.

Analysis: Similarity search against known "Risk Vectors" (Suicide, ADR, Misinformation).

Spatial-Temporal Mapping: Data is tagged with time and location metadata.

Output: Signals are pushed to the Sentinel Dashboard for human-in-the-loop verification.
